About Horse Electricals and Engineering Works

Horse Electrical Engineering Works is one of the best electrical leading companies in Ashanti Region. Horse Electrical Engineering Works from its incorporation in the last 3years has established itself as the Preferred Ally for Development in Ghana with operations in both Large and Small Scale construction with over 30 well trained employees under the able direction of executives and we know we are on the path of success.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

With the aim of achieving a strong leadership position in the engineering and construction industry by virtue of our commitment to excellence and quality through our on-time delivery, excellence services and exceeding client expectation, we pledge to deliver complete client satisfaction and a constructive contribution to our community through our work and accomplishments.

  • Reliability: Building long-term trust through consistent excellence.
